Animal Rescue Resources in the DMV

The Humane Rescue Alliance

The Humane Rescue Alliance is Washington D.C.'s largest rescue shelter, formed when the Washington Humane Society and the Washington Animal Rescue Leage organizations merged together.

  • Animal surrender and adoption
  • Foster programs
  • Veterinary care
  • Pet pantry
  • Animal Protection teams
  • Education programs

City Dog Rescue & City Kitties

City Dogs Rescue and its other division, City Kitties, is a registered non-profit network of DMV volunteers who rescue adoptable animals out of crowded shelters who are at risk of being euthanized with the goal of fostering and rehoming them. They run fostering programs and do not take surrenders on site. They have several types of opportunities to get involved!

  • Dog and cat adoptions
  • Foster program
  • Veterinary care

Located at

  • 301 H ST NE Washington, DC 20002
    • Contact at: operations@citydogsrescuedc.org

DC Paws Rescue

DC PAWS rescue is another registered non-profit volunteer and foster-based rescue in the DMV area. They rescue adoptable animals about to be euthanized and place them with foster families until they find a forever home. DC PAWS is currently working with a local rescuer in South Carolina who pulls adoptable dogs and cats from the local animal control facility and has the animals spayed/neutered and vetted. While awaiting transport to the DC area, the animals are cared for in a boarding facility where they receive daily interaction with area volunteers and participate in play groups.

  • Foster programs
  • Animal transportation
  • Spay and neuter

Contact at

  • info@dcpawsrescue.org

Homeward Trails Animal Rescue

Homeward Trails Animal Rescue is a registered non-profit animal rescue that places animals in the DC, Maryland, and Virginia area. Their area of operations extends throughout the Mid-Atlantic area and they focus a on rural shelters and owner surrenders.

  • Veterinary care and information
  • Spaying services
  • Education programs

Last Chance Animal Rescue

Last Chance Animal Rescue is a registered non-profit rescue that takes in cats and dogs from high kill shelters from over 12 states and fosters them in the DMV area. They also provide disaster relief services for pets and partner with Petco and PetSmart to help find forever homes.

  • Dog and cat adoption
  • Spay and neuter services
  • Foster programs
  • Partnered with PetCo and PetSmart locations
  • Dog walking volunteer opportunity
Located at
  • 8500 Bensville Rd, Waldorf, MD 20601

Rural Dog Rescue

Rural Dog Rescue is a volunteer based in Washington, D.C. that works with high kill shelters in Virginia, West Virginia, and North Carolina, Washington D.C., and Maryland to pull dogs and foster them rather than euthanization. They focus specifically on "underdogs" as opposed to "adoptables", including hospice, medical need, or breed specific discrimination.

  • Foster programs
  • Dog adoption
  • Dog training advice
  • Spay/neuter program
  • Animal transport
