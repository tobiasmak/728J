Recommended Watching

Hannah Shaw

Also known as the Kitten Lady, Hannah is a great resource to learn about kitten care. She specializes in cat rescue for kittens under two months old and has won multiple awards for her animal advocacy.

Her website, kittenlady.org, is a clearly organized site with all the basic information needed to serve this poplation made freely available.
She also runs a youtube channel that offers hundreds of hours of more kitten content.

Jackson Galaxy

Jackson Galaxy is a celebrated cat behaviouralist and tv show star, possibly most well known for his Animal Planet show My Cat From Hell. He's an excellent resource on cat behaviors and psychology and explaining "why does my cat do ___?"

Flatbush Cats

Flatbush Cat Rescue is a cat rescue based in Brooklyn, New York, focusing on the outdoor cat population in Flatbush. They produce youtube videos on the process of their Trap, Neuter, Release program and adoptions and are an excellent look at working feral rescue.
